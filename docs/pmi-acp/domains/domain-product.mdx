## Must‑know (5–7)
- Outcome vs output; định nghĩa value rõ; discovery & validation liên tục.
- Ưu tiên: MoSCoW, WSJF; hypothesis backlog.
- Roadmap thích ứng; cut scope theo giá trị.
- Đo lường value (NPS, adoption, revenue proxy); leading vs lagging.
- Story mapping; MVP/MLP; assumption tests.


## 5 bẫy đề
1) Lẫn lộn output = value.  
2) Ưu tiên theo tiếng nói lớn nhất.  
3) Không giả định/không kiểm chứng.  
4) Roadmap cứng, không thích ứng.  
5) Không có tiêu chí giá trị/DoD.

## Must‑know (5–7)
- Outcome vs output; clear value definition; continuous discovery/validation.
- Prioritization: MoSCoW, WSJF; hypothesis backlog.
- Adaptive roadmaps; value-based scope cuts.
- Value metrics (adoption, NPS, revenue proxy); leading/lagging.
- Story mapping; MVP/MLP; assumption tests.


## 5 pitfalls
1) Equating output with value.  
2) HiPPO-driven prioritization.  
3) Untested assumptions.  
4) Rigid roadmaps.  
5) Missing value criteria/DoD.

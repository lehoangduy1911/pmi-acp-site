## Must‑know (5–7)
- Dòng chảy ổn định; WIP limits; nhịp bền vững.
- Built‑in quality: TDD, Pairing, Code Review, DoD.
- Hiển thị rủi ro, impediments; quản trị kỹ thuật nợ.
- Quan sát luồng: CFD, cycle time, lead time.
- Liên tục hoá: CI/CD, small batch, trunk-based.


## 5 bẫy đề
1) Batch lớn, chờ đợi nhiều.  
2) Đẩy chất lượng về cuối.  
3) Không đo cycle/lead time.  
4) Ẩn rủi ro, nợ kỹ thuật.  
5) Over-commit vượt năng lực.

## Must‑know (5–7)
- Stable flow; WIP limits; sustainable pace.
- Built‑in quality: TDD, pairing, reviews, DoD.
- Visible risks/impediments; manage technical debt.
- Visualize flow: CFD, cycle time, lead time.
- Continuity: CI/CD, small batches, trunk-based.


## 5 pitfalls
1) Large batches & queues.  
2) Deferring quality.  
3) No cycle/lead metrics.  
4) Hidden risks/debt.  
5) Over-commitment.
